About

At Soka International School Malaysia , we believe that education is not solely about academic excellence, but about nurturing individuals who will make a meaningful impact on the world. The founding principles of our school is to develop global citizens of wisdom, courage, and compassion. These are the qualities the world urgently needs today, and they form the core values of our education and the foundation of our Global Citizenship Programme (GCP).

The GCP is designed to provide students with practical opportunities for self-discovery. Through a variety of experiential learning experiences, our students can explore their strengths and weaknesses and uncover their passions. These experiences go beyond acquiring knowledge or skills —they foster personal growth, encouraging students to step outside their comfort zones to truly understand the world today and the vital role they play in it.

The Global Citizenship Programme is a signature flagship program for SISM students. From the moment students join SISM in Year 7 all the way until they graduate in Year 13, students will have the opportunity to learn and participate in various activities specially tailored for them to become well-rounded Global Citizens. To add more meaning and motivation to the GCP, once they have completed each year group’s designated program, students are entitled to a Completion Badge and a Certificate of Completion.

Interview with Teacher Erjoe

1. What is the purpose of School GCP Programme?

SISM priotize global citizen, the school is trying to explore every corner as a global citizen. Explore every city and be aware of what is happening around the world. Prepare students for the world of tomorrow.

2. Which School GCP Programme was the most interesting one to you?

Y11 study abroad in Japan

3. Do you think the GCP project is actually helping students? why??

Yes, student's mindset changes after GCP project. The way they think, the way they speak.

4. Is the GCP project profiting SISM?

Yes, GCP is the chance for students to go outside of the classroom to learn something that u cannot learn in the class. To know what is the life outside of the school, the real-world problems. Understanding diverse cultures

Friendship, personal identity, communication skill, appreciative, learning how to manage, collaboration.

A group decision, always base on global citizen. Choosing nature as wanting students to appreciate the nature
